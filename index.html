About

Hi, I’m Gaby! I’m a web developer based out of Toronto. I create apps and websites that are functional and accessible and always keep my code DRY.

I have a Game Development background. Before enrolling in the immersive web development course at HackerYou, I worked as a 3D Artist for a small tech startup where, much by chance, I had the opportunity to use my knowledge of C#, the only programming language I knew at the time. I developed a simple VR experience for mobile,and quickly learned that I really liked to code!

When I’m not at the computer, I like to read and plan future travel adventures.

JQuery, JavaScript, CSS3, HTML5

Who will you stand beside in the ultimate showdown?

This quirky little quiz is my very first JavaScript app. I used JS and JQuery to add points to a tally that, after answering a few questions, will give you one of two results. If the points are tied, the final result will be randomly chosen. I used namespacing to keep it organized.
